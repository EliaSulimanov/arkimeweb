---
title: Truncate TLS
description: 'Rule that will only save the first 20 packets of TLS sessions. The packets will still be counted and processed, they are just not saved to disk.'
tags: tls truncate fieldSet
---

```
  - name: "Truncate TLS PCAP"
    when: "fieldSet"
    fields:
      protocols:
      - tls
    ops:
      _maxPacketsToSave: 20
```
