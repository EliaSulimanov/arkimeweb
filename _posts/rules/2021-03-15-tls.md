---
title: Set Protocols
description: 'If the protocols field is set to tls and the host.http is set to www.aol.com, mail.google.com, or foo.bar.com, then add tlsrulestest to the protocols field.'
tags: tls setfield
---

```
  - name: "Set tlsrulestest on certain hosts"
    when: "fieldSet"
    fields:
      protocols:
        - tls
      host.http:
        - www.aol.com
        - mail.google.com
        - foo.bar.com
    ops:
      "protocols": "tlsrulestest"
```
