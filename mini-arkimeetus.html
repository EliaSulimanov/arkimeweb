Mini Arkimeetus

Calling all Arkimists! Join us twice a month for open office hours, where you can chat and ask questions with the Arkime developers and other Arkime folks. These mini meetups will be fairly informal. The current plan is to have some with no set agenda, just come and chat, while others will have planned demos and presentations.

We will be hosting them on the first and third Wednesdays of each month at 2pm Eastern (UTC-5)
We will be using Blue Jeans Video Conferencing Technology - which offers both a web and downloadable client.

    Guest Speakers: Titaniam

    Titaniam provides privacy compliance and data protection for Arkime that stays on even as data is accessed and manipulated. It does not reveal clear text even if the host is accessed directly. Titaniam will be demonstrating how you can protect IP and MAC addresses, hostnames and other data in Arkime without interfering with functionality.
